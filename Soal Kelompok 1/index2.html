Tic-tac-toe (American English), noughts and crosses (Commonwealth English), or Xs and Os (Irish English) is a paper-and-pencil game for two players who take turns marking the spaces in a three-by-three grid with X or O . The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner. It is a game, with a forced draw assuming best play from both players.
